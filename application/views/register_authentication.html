Last Step: Verify Your Identity

We need to make sure only you can access your private pharmacy records and health information. To do so, we will need you to verify your identity using one of the convenient options below.

Afterwards you'll have full access to your pharmacy account, including prescription refills, prescription history and more.

  • Online
  • By Phone

Verify Online (recommended)

Verify your identity right now by answering a few simple questions about yourself. They are generated using personal identifying information, such as your date of birth. This information is used only to confirm your identity. This secure process is very similar to those used by banks and credit institutions. It is designed to keep your information secure and prevent unauthorized individuals from accessing your personal information.

Verify by Phone

Our automated system is easy to use and will take only a few moments of your time.

3771 0089
For faster verification, please call from the phone number the pharmacy has on file.

After Verification

Once you've completed your verification, use the Access Your Account button below to continue. From there you'll be able to access prescription refills, prescription history and more.

Verify Later

If you need to complete your verification later, you can still order a prescription refill using In-store Express Refills.
Note: Make sure you have the prescription number you wish to refill before using In-store Express Refills.
